More about Stoisicm

To continue your journey and find out more about the ancient art of stoicism here are some of the best books and podcasts with links

Essential Reading

Meditations

by Marcus Aurelius
Written in Greek by the only Roman emperor who was also a philosopher, without any intention of publication, the Meditations of Marcus Aurelius offer a remarkable series of challenging spiritual reflections and exercises developed as the emperor struggled to understand himself and make sense of the universe
buy on amazon audio version

The Obstacle Is the Way

by Ryan Holiday
We are stuck, stymied, frustrated. But it needn’t be this way. There is a formula for success that’s been followed by the icons of history—from John D. Rockefeller to Amelia Earhart to Ulysses S. Grant to Steve Jobs—a formula that let them turn obstacles into opportunities.
buy on amazon audio version

The Daily Stoic

by Ryan Holiday
Modern readers praise Stoic philosophy for its unique blend of practicality and wisdom. But it's admittedly hard for the average reader to decipher the Dover Thrift edition of Marcus Aurelius' work. The antiquated, needlessly formal language of most modern translation.
buy on amazon audio version

The Beginner's Guide to Stoicism

by Matthew Van Natta
With this stoicism guide, the beginner stoic will learn how to take charge of their emotions on the path to sustained happiness and satisfaction. This easy-to-navigate stoicism guide gives you the emotional tools needed to let go of the things you can't control and find joy in what you have
buy on amazon audio version

The Best Stoticism Podcasts

If you’re an avid podcast listener and haver become interested in the Stoics, you’re probably always on the look out for good Stoicism podcasts to plug into. Give these a try

Good Fortune

Host: Matthew Van Natta
Website: https://immoderatestoic.com/good-fortune/
Although there haven’t been any new episodes of Good Fortune for a while, the great thing about this type of content is that it doesn’t get outdated, so there’s plenty of useful stuff in the currently available episodes.

The Daily Stoic

Host: Ryan Holiday
Website: https://dailystoic.com/podcast/
Hosted by author Ryan Holiday, a brilliant podcast called The Daily Stoic. On weekdays, the podcast is the short audio companion to Daily Stoic’s daily emails. On weekends, longer-form episodes such as interviews are published.

The Tim Ferris Show

Host: Tim Ferris
Website: https://tim.blog/tag/stoicism/
Another podcast worth a listen is The Tim Ferriss Show. While it’s not solely based on Stoicism, there are plenty of episodes on that specific topic.

The Sunday Stoic Podcast

Host: Steve Karafiat
Website: https://www.sundaystoicpodcast.com//
Sunday Stoic is another podcast focusing on readings of the ancient Stoics and the modern applications of the lessons learnt. As you can probably guess, new episodes are published on Sundays!
